# Email Variants Review & Guidelines

## Summary

All email variants have been reviewed and updated to follow comprehensive guidelines that ensure:
- **Empathy-driven** messaging showing we understand because we've been there
- **Peer support** emphasis - we're supporting our community
- **Startup value** - we're a small team that values peer recommendations
- **Importance** - this is very important to many people who need it
- **Positive impressions** - makes recipients feel good and valued
- **No pressure** - always emphasizes it's just a resource share
